Save The Wildlife

A wild animal is an animal that is, well, wild. This means that it isn't tame and it lives on its own without any help from people. A wild animal finds its own food, shelter, water and all its other needs in a specific natural habitat. Habitat can be a field, woods, pond, wetland, prairie, park, or your yard.

List of big wild animals found in India are The Royal Bengal Tiger, Asiatic Elephant, Great Indian Rhino, The Asiatic Lion, Indian Leopard, Indian giant squirrel, Indian wild Bore, Indian wild Ass, Black Buck, and Nilgai.

Problems

Hunters cause injuries, pain and suffering to animals who are not adapted to defend themselves from bullets, traps and other cruel killing devices.

Hunting destroys animal families and habitats, and leaves terrified and dependent baby animals behind to starve to death.

Solutions

How can we prvent the Wild Animals from getting injured?

Volunteer. If you don't have money to give, donate your time.

Visit. Zoos, aquariums, national parks and wildlife refuges are all home to wild animals.

Plant native species. Creating planet- and wildlife-friendly environments can start right in your own backyard and in your community.

Donate to organizations created to help our zoo animals and wildlife

Importance

Importance of Wildlife

Animal, plant and marine biodiversity keeps ecosystems functional.

Healthy ecosystems allow us to survive, get enough food to eat and make a living.

When species disappear or fall in number, ecosystems and people—especially the world's poorest—suffer.

For many, people living in or near the forest, wildlife is the source of income and provides them with their daily bread and butter.
